About Us

Welcome to Matoshree Institute of Paramedical Sciences,Murum

Matoshree Institute of Paramedical Sciences,Murum, popularly known as MIPS in the Education sector is equipped with Advanced Studies Courses and was established in Murum, Omarga with the objective of providing quality Paramedical care education for 10th, 10+2, graduate and post graduate levels. Young aspirants study in a stimulating environment of independent thinking and innovation in various disciplines of Health Care, Hospitality and self-employment.MIPS is focused on value addition to the courses that it offers to the student community and provides the unique combination of Health excellence, opportunities, choices and experience. With rapid globalization of all economic activities, it is imperative that students develop a global outlook to become successful in the International arena.The Institution is working for promotion of the Paramedical Health Education in the Country. This Institution is working towards creating a band of well trained Paramedical Professionals.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, comprehensive paramedical education that empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical values necessary for successful careers in healthcare.

Our Vision

“To develop the lateral thinking capacity of the students. Train them to be the leaders and responsible citizen who would be ready to work untiringly towards the betterment of the country”.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in education and training.
  • Integrity: We uphold ethical principles and professional conduct.
  • Compassion: We foster a culture of caring and empathy towards patients and the community.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and teaching methodologies to enhance learning.
  • Collaboration: We work together with students, faculty, and the healthcare industry to achieve our goals.

We believe in creating a supportive and stimulating learning environment where students can thrive and reach their full potential. Our experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and industry partnerships ensure that our graduates are well-prepared to meet the challenges and opportunities of the healthcare field.

Join us at Matoshree Institute of Paramedical Sciences and embark on a rewarding journey towards a fulfilling career in paramedical sciences.
